🚀 How It Works - 4 Simple Steps

Getting your EV scooter from ZiGo Mobility is incredibly simple and fast!

1

📍 Go to Nearest Hub

Visit our nearest ZiGo Mobility hub location in Zirakpur. Open 6 AM - 10 PM daily.

2

📱 Scan QR Code

Use your phone to scan the QR code on the scooter. Get instant access details and ride information.

3

✅ Verify Documents

Show your original Driving License and Aadhar card. Quick verification (2-3 minutes) and you're ready!

4

🎯 Ride & Return

Ride to your destination and return to any ZiGo hub. Pay based on distance - done!

📋 Complete Process Details:

  • Step 1 - Locate Hub: Find your nearest ZiGo Mobility hub. All major areas of Zirakpur will be covered.
  • Step 2 - Scan QR: Use any smartphone to scan the QR code on the EV scooter. Takes 5 seconds.
  • Step 3 - Verify:
    • Show Original Driving License (DL)
    • Show Original Aadhar Card
    • Quick verification takes 2-3 minutes
    • Payment mode confirmation (Cash/UPI)
  • Step 4 - Start Ride: Get the scooter keys, check battery level, and start your journey!
  • Step 5 - Return: Return to any ZiGo hub. Our staff will note the distance and calculate fare.
📋 Documents Required:
  • ✓ Original Driving License (DL) - Must be valid two-wheeler DL
  • ✓ Original Aadhar Card - Government ID verification
  • ✓ Age: Minimum 18 years old
  • ✓ Helmet: Provided free by us (or bring your own)
